Overview

This privacy policy explains how the Food Truck Finder mobile application ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your personal information, particularly regarding camera permissions required by Google Play Store policies.

Camera Permission Usage

Important: This app requests camera permission ONLY for legitimate business purposes related to food truck management and customer experience.

Why We Need Camera Access

  • Food Truck Business Photos: Owners can photograph their trucks, food items, and daily menus
  • Profile Pictures: Users can take profile photos for their food truck listings
  • Menu Documentation: Capture images of menu boards, daily specials, and food preparations
  • Business Verification: Help verify legitimate food truck businesses through visual documentation

Data Collection and Handling

What Data We Collect

  • Photos: Only when you explicitly choose to take or upload them
  • Location Data: To help customers find nearby food trucks
  • Account Information: Name, email, business name (for food truck owners)
  • Usage Data: App performance and crash reports (anonymous)

How We Protect Your Data

  • Secure Storage: Photos stored on Cloudinary CDN with encryption
  • No Background Access: Camera is only accessed when you actively use photo features
  • User Control: You can delete photos and revoke permissions anytime
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Photos are not shared with advertisers or data brokers

Permission Details

Camera Permission (android.permission.CAMERA)

  • When: Only when you tap camera/photo buttons in the app
  • Purpose: Take photos for food truck business listings and profiles
  • Storage: Photos saved only with your explicit consent
  • Control: Can be revoked in Android Settings > Apps > Food Truck Finder > Permissions

Location Permissions

  • Purpose: Find nearby food trucks and help owners share their location
  • Background: Only when explicitly enabled for real-time truck tracking
  • Accuracy: Approximate location sufficient for food truck finding

Your Rights and Controls

  • Revoke Permissions: Disable camera/location access in device settings
  • Delete Photos: Remove uploaded photos from your profile anytime
  • Account Deletion: Request complete data removal
  • Data Export: Request a copy of your data
  • Opt-Out: Stop location tracking or photo features

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We DO NOT share your personal data with:

  • Advertising networks
  • Data brokers or analytics companies
  • Social media platforms (unless you explicitly share)
  • Any third parties for marketing purposes

We may share data only when:

  • You explicitly make your food truck listing public
  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to protect our rights or user safety
  • With service providers under strict confidentiality (hosting, security)

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately.

Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption for data transmission
  • Secure cloud storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited employee access to personal data
